# Grammar and Style Enhancer

## Purpose
Improves writing quality by checking grammar, enhancing style, and ensuring consistency.

## Input
- Text to review
- Target style guide (AP, Chicago, etc.)
- Genre conventions
- Author's voice preferences

## Process
1. Check grammar and punctuation
2. Identify awkward phrasing
3. Suggest stronger word choices
4. Ensure consistent tense and POV
5. Check dialogue formatting
6. Enhance readability and flow
7. Maintain author's unique voice

## Output
- Corrected text
- Style suggestions
- Readability score
- Tone analysis
- List of changes made

## Example Usage
```
Review and enhance chapter 5, maintaining a suspenseful tone and active voice.
```

## Best Practices
- Preserve author's unique voice
- Consider genre conventions
- Balance correctness with creativity
- Prioritize clarity and impact
